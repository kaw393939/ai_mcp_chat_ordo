# Chapter 9 — AI and Accessibility: Automated Remediation and Assistive Technology

## Abstract

AI creates both opportunities and risks for accessibility. This chapter covers AI-powered alt text generation, automated ARIA annotation, real-time captioning, and the risk of AI-generated content that is itself inaccessible.

---

## AI as Accessibility Tool

- **Automated alt text**: AI generates image descriptions (requires human review for accuracy)
- **Real-time captioning**: speech-to-text for meetings and video content
- **Voice interfaces**: natural language interaction for users who cannot use visual interfaces
- **Content simplification**: AI-assisted plain language translation for cognitive accessibility

## AI Accessibility Risks

- AI-generated images without alt text
- AI-generated content without heading structure
- Chatbot interfaces that are keyboard-inaccessible
- AI decisions that discriminate against users with disabilities

## Chapter Checklist

- Is AI-generated content reviewed for accessibility before publication?
- Do your AI-powered interfaces (chat, voice) meet WCAG requirements?
- Are you using AI tools to enhance accessibility (captioning, alt text)?
