# Chapter 6 — Content Governance: Standards and Workflows

## Abstract

Content governance ensures that content quality is maintained over time. This chapter covers editorial workflows, approval processes, and the discipline of content retirement.

## The Content Lifecycle

1. **Plan**: identify the need
2. **Create**: write, design, and produce
3. **Review**: editorial and legal review
4. **Publish**: deploy to production
5. **Maintain**: update as needed
6. **Retire**: remove when no longer relevant

## Chapter Checklist

- Does every piece of content have a named owner?
- Is there a content review process before publication?
- When was the last time you retired outdated content?
