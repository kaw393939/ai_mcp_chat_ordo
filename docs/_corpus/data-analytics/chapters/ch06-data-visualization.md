# Chapter 6 — Data Visualization: Making Numbers Legible

## Abstract

Data visualization is the translation of numbers into perceptual form. This chapter applies Tufte's principles (Book III, Chapter 2) to analytics dashboards: maximize data-ink ratio, minimize chartjunk, and choose the right chart type for the right question.

## Chart Type Selection

| Question | Best Chart |
| ---------- | ----------- |
| How does X change over time? | Line chart |
| How does X compare across categories? | Bar chart |
| What is the distribution of X? | Histogram |
| What is the relationship between X and Y? | Scatter plot |
| What proportion of the whole is X? | Stacked bar (not pie chart) |

## Chapter Checklist

- Does every chart have a clear title stating the insight it conveys?
- Have you eliminated decorative elements (3D effects, gradient backgrounds)?
- Can a stakeholder understand your dashboard in under 30 seconds?
