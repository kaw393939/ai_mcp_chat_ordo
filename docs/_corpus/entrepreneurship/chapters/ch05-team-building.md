# Chapter 5 — Team Building: The First Ten Hires

## Abstract

The first ten employees define a company's culture, capability, and trajectory. This chapter covers the founder-team dynamic, the first hire dilemma, and the cultural patterns that scale.

## Key Principle

Hire for strengths that complement your weaknesses, not for people who think like you. The most successful founding teams cover three capabilities: building the product (engineering), selling the product (marketing/sales), and managing the product (operations/strategy).

## Chapter Checklist

- Do your first hires complement your weaknesses?
- Is your hiring process values-aligned, not just skills-aligned?
