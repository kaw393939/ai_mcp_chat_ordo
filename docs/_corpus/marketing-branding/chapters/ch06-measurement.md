# Chapter 6 — Marketing Measurement: Attribution and ROI

## Abstract

Marketing without measurement is spending without accountability. This chapter covers attribution models, the difference between vanity and actionable marketing metrics, and the discipline of connecting marketing activity to revenue.

## Key Metrics

- **CAC**: Customer Acquisition Cost
- **ROAS**: Return on Ad Spend
- **MQL → SQL conversion**: Marketing Qualified Lead to Sales Qualified Lead
- **Attribution**: which touchpoints contributed to conversion

## Chapter Checklist

- Can you calculate your blended CAC?
- Do you have an attribution model (even imperfect)?
- Are marketing metrics tied to revenue outcomes?
