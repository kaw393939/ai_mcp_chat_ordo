# Chapter 7 — Community Building: Customers as Co-Creators

## Abstract

A community is not an audience. An audience consumes; a community contributes. This chapter covers community-led growth, user-generated content, and the operational discipline of community management.

## Chapter Checklist

- Does your product have an active community (forum, Discord, Slack)?
- Are community members creating content or helping other users?
- Is community health measured and invested in?
