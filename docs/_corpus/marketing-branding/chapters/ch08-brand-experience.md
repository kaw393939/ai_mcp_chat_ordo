# Chapter 8 — Brand Experience: Every Touchpoint Is a Promise

## Abstract

Brand experience extends beyond marketing to every touchpoint: customer support, billing, error messages, packaging, and the product itself. This chapter covers experience consistency and the discipline of managing brand across all channels.

## Chapter Checklist

- Is your brand voice consistent across marketing, product, and support?
- Does your error messaging reflect your brand personality?
- Would a customer recognize your brand from a support interaction alone?
