## Critical Issues (must fix before publication)

1. **The Five-Stage Loop is underdeveloped.** Lines 68–82: the chapter's title concept receives only one sentence per stage. Meanwhile Phase Zero spans ~50 lines. Each stage needs at least a short paragraph describing *how* it works with concrete guidance.

2. **"Deterministic and Probabilistic Verification in Tandem" is misplaced.** Lines 42–54 are nested under Phase Zero but describe verification during execution; this logically belongs under or adjacent to Stage 4 (Verify).

3. **Video rendering example is unconnected to the book's subject.** Lines 31–33 and 49–50 introduce a domain appearing nowhere else. Either replace with a domain-relevant example (the LLM chat system) or explicitly state the example shows technique generalization.

## Substantive Concerns (strongly recommend fixing)

1. **Phase Zero scope creep.** The section expands into verification strategy, sculpting metaphors, and domain generalization beyond its stated purpose. Consider splitting into sub-sections or moving verification content out.

2. **Model note on context amnesia is disconnected.** Lines 117–119: follows Anti-Patterns rather than the Archive stage where it has maximum impact. Move it immediately after the Archive discussion.

3. **Repository Example doesn't show the loop in motion.** Lines 87–93 name directories but don't walk through one concrete cycle. A worked example would demonstrate the loop concretely.

4. **Voice boundary blurred.** Line 37: first-person model voice appears inside a numbered list before the formal model note block. Keep the list in author voice.

## Minor Issues (copyedit level)

1. Line 54: the video example re-anchoring is missing; reader may lose thread across subsections.
2. Line 83: *"memory architecture"* is undefined and used nowhere else. Use a more transparent phrase.
3. Line 5: Abstract pre-enumerates the five stages — consider not repeating what the chapter lays out in full.
4. Inconsistent tab indentation in the Five-Stage Loop list (lines 69–81).

## Strengths (what works well)

- Phase Zero (Inquiry-Based Intelligence Gathering) is the chapter's most original contribution.
- "Intelligence is concentrated per phase" is a novel and valuable framing.
- Model note on context amnesia is one of the best passages in the book.
- Anti-patterns section is precise and recognizable.
