## Substantive Concerns (strongly recommend fixing)

1. **CEO Operating Model section is disproportionately long.** ~40–50% of the chapter. The core Team Operating Model content (roles, rituals, handoff contracts, anti-patterns) is compressed. Consider rebalancing or splitting.

2. **Video rendering example is hypothetical.** The passage is vivid but has no corresponding repository artifact. Flag explicitly as hypothetical or supplement with a real repo example.

3. **"Limits of the CEO model" conflates two distinct problems.** Underspecified objectives produce wrong output; absent verification produces *undetected* wrong output. These compound differently and deserve separate subsections.

4. **Core Rituals section is skeletal.** Four rituals at one sentence each. These are the operational core and need examples showing what artifacts look like in practice.
