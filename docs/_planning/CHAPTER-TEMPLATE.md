## Notes for Authors

- **Target word count:** 8,000–12,000 words
- **Practitioner profiles:** Minimum 500 words each
- **"What frustrated them":** Required. One sentence. Present tense or past tense.
- **Code examples:** At minimum 1 executable example with before/after
- **Checklist:** 3–5 items, each verifiable by the reader
- **Cross-references:** Link to related chapters in other books where applicable
- **Tone:** Direct, practitioner-to-practitioner. Not academic. Not marketing copy.
