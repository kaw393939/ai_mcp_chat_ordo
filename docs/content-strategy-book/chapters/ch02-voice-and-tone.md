# Chapter 2 — Voice and Tone: Consistent Personality Across Contexts

## Abstract
Voice is who you are. Tone is how you adapt to context. This chapter covers voice and tone documentation, the Mailchimp Content Style Guide as an industry standard, and the discipline of writing consistently across hundreds of pages and dozens of contributors.

## Mailchimp's Content Style Guide

**Mailchimp** published their Content Style Guide publicly, establishing a model for voice and tone documentation. Their framework:
- **Voice**: the consistent personality (friendly, expert, human, not corporate)
- **Tone**: the contextual adaptation (celebratory on success screens, empathetic on error screens, direct on billing pages)

## Chapter Checklist
- Is your voice documented in a style guide?
- Does your tone adapt to context (errors, success, billing, marketing)?
- Can a new writer apply your voice correctly using your documentation?
