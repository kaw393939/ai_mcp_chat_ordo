# Chapter 7 — Content Measurement: What Gets Read

## Abstract
Content measurement answers: is this content achieving its goal? This chapter covers content performance metrics, the distinction between engagement metrics and outcome metrics, and the discipline of measuring content ROI.

## Key Metrics
| Metric | Measures |
|--------|----------|
| Page views | Reach (how many people saw it) |
| Time on page | Engagement (did they read it?) |
| Scroll depth | Completion (how far did they get?) |
| Conversion rate | Outcome (did they take the desired action?) |
| Search ranking | Discoverability (can people find it?) |

## Chapter Checklist
- Can you measure the business outcome of your top 10 content assets?
- Are you tracking scroll depth and time on page (not just page views)?
- Is content performance reviewed and acted on regularly?
