# Chapter 9 — AI-Augmented Content: Generation, Personalization, and Quality

## Abstract
AI transforms content operations: drafting, editing, personalizing, and translating at scales previously impossible. This chapter covers AI content workflows, quality assurance for AI-generated content, and the intelligence concentration thesis applied to content strategy.

## The Intelligence Concentration Thesis
AI generates content broadly and cheaply. But undifferentiated AI content — generic, unfocused, and voice-inconsistent — is noise. The content strategist's role is to *concentrate* AI energy into focused, voice-consistent, audience-specific content that sounds human because it was *directed* by a human with domain expertise.

## Chapter Checklist
- Is AI-generated content reviewed and edited by a human before publication?
- Does AI-generated content follow your documented voice and tone?
- Are you using AI for first drafts, or for finished output?
