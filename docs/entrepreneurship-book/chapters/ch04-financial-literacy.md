# Chapter 4 — Financial Literacy: Cash, Burn, and Runway

## Abstract
Most startups die not from lack of product-market fit but from running out of money. This chapter covers the financial fundamentals every founder must understand: cash flow, burn rate, runway, and the discipline of unit economics.

## Key Concepts
- **Burn rate**: monthly cash expenditure
- **Runway**: cash reserves ÷ burn rate = months until death
- **Break-even**: the point where revenue covers expenses
- **Gross margin**: revenue minus cost of goods sold, divided by revenue

## Chapter Checklist
- Do you know your current monthly burn rate?
- Do you know your runway in months?
- Can you articulate the path to break-even?
