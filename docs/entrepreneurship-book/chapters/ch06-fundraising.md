# Chapter 6 — Fundraising: Capital as Fuel, Not as Goal

## Abstract
Raising capital is not a milestone — it is a tool. This chapter covers the fundraising landscape, the venture capital model, bootstrapping versus funding, and the discipline of raising only what you need.

## The Fundraising Spectrum
| Approach | Best For | Tradeoff |
|----------|----------|----------|
| Bootstrapping | Profitable from day one | Slower growth |
| Angel investment | Pre-product, pre-revenue | Dilution, small checks |
| Venture capital | Proven PMF, ready to scale | Significant dilution, growth pressure |
| Revenue-based financing | Steady revenue, want to avoid dilution | Repayment obligations |

## Chapter Checklist
- Can you articulate why you need external capital (vs. bootstrapping)?
- Do you know how much dilution you're accepting — and is it worth it?
