# Chapter 7 — Growth: From First Customer to Market

## Abstract
Growth is not marketing. Growth is the systematic expansion of value delivery. This chapter covers growth loops, network effects, and the discipline of scaling what works rather than exploring what might.

## Growth Loops
A growth loop is a self-reinforcing cycle: user signs up → uses product → creates content → content attracts new users → new user signs up. Unlike a funnel (linear), a loop is circular — each cycle produces inputs for the next.

## Chapter Checklist
- Can you diagram your primary growth loop?
- Is your growth organic (product-driven) or paid (marketing-driven)?
- What is your viral coefficient (how many new users does each existing user bring)?
