# Chapter 8 — Operational Discipline: Systems That Scale

## Abstract
The skills that get a startup from 0 to 1 are different from the skills that get it from 1 to 100. This chapter covers the operational systems that enable scaling: documentation, process design, and the transition from founder-driven decisions to system-driven decisions.

## Key Transition
Founders make decisions by judgment. Scaled organizations make decisions by process. The founder's job is to encode their judgment into processes that others can execute — then get out of the way.

## Chapter Checklist
- Are your critical processes documented?
- Can operations continue if any single person is unavailable?
