# Chapter 9 — AI-Native Entrepreneurship: The New Leverage

## Abstract
AI has collapsed the cost of building software by 10x or more. This changes entrepreneurship fundamentally: solo founders can build products that previously required ten-person teams. This chapter covers the AI-native startup stack, the new competitive dynamics, and the intelligence concentration thesis applied to entrepreneurial execution.

## The Intelligence Concentration Thesis Applied
The thesis of this series — that concentrated energy becomes intelligence — is directly applicable to entrepreneurship. A founder who gives an AI a vague directive ("build me an app") gets a generic result. A founder who concentrates their intelligence into precise specifications, domain constraints, and quality criteria gets a product that would have taken months to build manually.

The founder's competitive advantage is no longer *technical skill* alone — it is *taste, judgment, and domain expertise*. AI provides the execution leverage. The founder provides the direction.

## Chapter Checklist
- Are you using AI to amplify your strengths, or to compensate for missing skills?
- Is your competitive moat in your domain knowledge, not in your code?
- Can you articulate what your AI-augmented workflow produces that a non-augmented competitor cannot?
