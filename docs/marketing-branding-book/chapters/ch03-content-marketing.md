# Chapter 3 — Content Marketing: Teaching as Marketing

## Abstract
Content marketing replaces interruption with attraction. Instead of pushing messages to people who don't want them, content marketing creates valuable material that pulls interested people toward the brand.

## Joe Pulizzi and Content Inc. (2015)

**Joe Pulizzi** (Content Marketing Institute) formalized content marketing as a business strategy. His framework: build an audience through valuable content *before* selling to them. The content earns attention; the product earns revenue.

## Chapter Checklist
- Does your content provide genuine value independent of your product?
- Is content creation a consistent habit (weekly minimum)?
- Can you measure the pipeline contribution of content marketing?
