# Chapter 4 — Storytelling Frameworks: Narrative as Strategy

## Abstract
Humans remember stories better than facts. This chapter covers storytelling frameworks for marketing: Donald Miller's StoryBrand, the hero's journey applied to customer narratives, and the discipline of making the customer the hero — not the product.

## Donald Miller and StoryBrand (2017)

**Donald Miller** published *Building a StoryBrand*, applying Joseph Campbell's hero's journey to marketing. His framework: the customer is the hero. The brand is the guide. The guide has a plan. The plan leads to success. This clarifies messaging by removing the brand from the center of the story.

## Chapter Checklist
- Is your customer the hero of your marketing story, or is your product?
- Can you articulate your customer's problem, desire, and fear in one sentence each?
- Does your homepage follow the StoryBrand framework?
