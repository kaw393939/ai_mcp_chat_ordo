# Chapter 5 — Digital Marketing Channels: Where Attention Lives

## Abstract
Channel strategy is the discipline of being where your customers already are. This chapter covers SEO, paid acquisition, email, social media, and the discipline of channel selection based on customer behavior.

## Channel Selection Framework
| Channel | Best For | Measurement |
|---------|----------|-------------|
| SEO | Intent-driven discovery | Organic traffic, keyword rankings |
| Paid search | High-intent capture | CPA, ROAS |
| Social media | Awareness, community | Engagement rate, reach |
| Email | Nurture, retention | Open rate, click rate, revenue per email |
| Content | Trust, authority | Time on page, backlinks, form fills |

## Chapter Checklist
- Are your channel investments proportional to where your customers spend attention?
- Can you measure ROI per channel?
- Are you in fewer than 5 channels (focus > presence)?
