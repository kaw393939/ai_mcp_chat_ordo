# Chapter 9 — AI-Augmented Marketing: Personalization at Scale

## Abstract
AI enables marketing personalization that was previously impossible: dynamic content, predictive segmentation, automated testing, and individual-level messaging. This chapter covers the opportunities, the ethical boundaries, and the intelligence concentration thesis applied to marketing.

## The Intelligence Concentration Thesis
Broad marketing (same message to everyone) wastes most of its energy on non-prospects. AI enables *concentrated* marketing: the right message to the right person at the right moment. This is intelligence concentration applied to attention — focusing energy where it creates the most value.

## Chapter Checklist
- Are you using AI for content personalization?
- Is your segmentation based on behavior, not just demographics?
- Are ethical boundaries defined for personalization (opt-out, transparency)?
